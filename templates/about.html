    Story behind Nexus Web Tools

    Before Howard became a web developer, he worked as a SEO specialist at a local marketing company.

    As any computer programmer would, he always asked himself, "How can we do things better, faster, and easier?" He could not believe the level of tediousness some of these SEO work entailed. A firm believer of automation, Howard started writing script to automate some of his SEO work.

    His first script was that downloaded hundreds of pictures when he was asked to manually download them from Flickr. His second script was a bit more complex. Using a spreadsheet data of thousands of potentially spammy incoming links from various other websites that were harming the SEO value of our client site, he wrote a script that automatically generated list of untrustworthy sites (disavow list) that could be submitted to Google. (More about disavowing links here.)
